About this tool

VIV PPM Calculator v3.0

Real-time prediction of patient-prosthesis mismatch (PPM) during valve-in-valve transcatheter aortic valve implantation (VIV-TAVI), based on a hybrid Gorlin approach combining echocardiographic stroke volume with intra-procedural invasive hemodynamics.

Method: AVA = Mean systolic aortic pressure / (Zao × 44.3 × √ΔPmean), where Zao is calibrated at baseline using echo-derived stroke volume.

PPM thresholds: ESC/EACTS 2025 guidelines.

BSA formula
DuBois (default): BSA = 0.007184 × W^0.425 × H^0.725. Mosteller: BSA = √(W × H / 3600), simpler and performs better in extreme body sizes.
